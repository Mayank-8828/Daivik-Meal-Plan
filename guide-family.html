Welcome to Daivik's
food journey

A little window into what he's eating and how he's growing
made with love · for family

Hi 💛 Mayank and Sneha set up this little app so the whole family — dadi, dada, nana, nani, mami, mama, maasi, foi — anyone who loves Daivik can peek in any time and see how his meals are going.

You don't need to do anything. You can't accidentally change anything. Just open it like a photo album: see what he ate today, which new fruits he's tried, his cute meal photos, and how big he's getting. 🌱

1 Getting in

Opening the app on your phone

📱

The link & PIN

Open mealplan.mayankkariya.com on your phone's browser. Tap the 👪 Family card and type the 4-digit PIN that Mayank shared with you.

That's it. The app stays open — you don't need to sign in again every time.

⭐

Add it to your home screen (optional)

So you can tap it like any other app:

  1. Open the link in Chrome (Android) or Safari (iPhone)
  2. Tap the menu (three dots) or share button
  3. Choose "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Name it "Daivik" 💕

2 What you'll see

The five little tabs

🏠

Today

What Daivik is eating today — morning, afternoon, evening meals. With a green tick next to anything he's already had. Look here first 💛

📅

Plan

The whole 7-week diet plan, week by week. Tap a day to see exactly what was given. Tap a meal to see cute photos Mama clicked, and her notes — "he grabbed the spoon today!" 😍

🥬

Foods

A list of every vegetable, dal and fruit. The ones he's tried get a happy green mark. Watching this list grow over weeks is so satisfying.

📊

Insights

Pretty charts: how many new foods he's tried, his moods, and best of all — his growth chart 📏. You'll see how much weight and length he's added since starting solids.

👶

Profile

Daivik's photo, his age in months, his latest weight and length, and his weaning milestones. The sweet stuff.

4 A little reassurance

You can't break anything

💝

This is view-only

Tap anywhere you want. Open any meal, scroll through every week, zoom into photos — there's no edit button on your account. Only Mama and Papa can add or change anything. You're free to explore.

🌸 A small ask If you notice something sweet — Daivik tried a new fruit, gained weight, learned a new skill — feel free to message Mama directly. She'll love it. The app doesn't have a chat feature, that's on purpose.

5 If you wonder...

Little questions

I tapped a button and nothing happened. Did I do something wrong?

No 💛 The edit buttons are hidden for you, but some buttons (like marking a meal "fed") simply don't react when tapped. That's by design — only Mama and Papa can change those.

Will Mama or Papa know if I checked the app?

No. There's no activity tracking. You can look at any time, any number of times, without anyone seeing.

Can I save a photo to my phone?

Tap a meal photo to open it bigger, then long-press to save it to your phone — works on iPhone and Android.

What if I forget the PIN?

Just ask Mayank — he can remind you or change it. No drama.

Can I use it on a computer too?

Yes — open the same link on your laptop's browser. Works everywhere.

Will it cost anything?

No — completely free for you to use.
