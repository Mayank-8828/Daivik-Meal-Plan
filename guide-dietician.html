A shared, real-time view of how Daivik is actually eating this week — and a place where you can shape next week's plan directly.
Daivik · born 6 Oct 2025 · started solids 4 April 2026

Thank you for using this app with us 💚 We built it so you can see exactly what's happening on the ground between consultations — and so you can edit the plan directly instead of going through emails and Excel attachments.

Your account is a planner account — you can:

  • See everything Mama and Papa log, in real time (usually under a second).
  • Edit, move, delete, or add any meal on any day, in any week.
  • Add a brand new week manually, or import one from an Excel sheet.
  • Add or remove items from the master food list (vegetables, dals, fruits).
  • Flag a food long-press if it should be avoided.

You cannot mark meals as "fed", add quantities/mood/photos/notes (that's Mama and Papa's daily log), or change Daivik's growth measurements and profile photo. The app hides those buttons for you so the daily-log surface stays simple.

This guide takes 4 minutes. Section 3 is the new and most useful one — it walks through every editing tool you have.

1 Getting in

Signing in & staying signed in

📱

Where to find it

Open mealplan.mayankkariya.com in your browser. On the welcome screen, tap the 🩺 Dietician card and enter the 4-digit PIN we shared with you.

You stay signed in until you sign out — works on phone, tablet, laptop. Add it to your home screen for a one-tap launch (browser menu → "Add to Home Screen").

2 What you can see

The five tabs, at a glance

🏠

Today

What Daivik is supposed to eat today, with a tick next to each meal that's been fed. Has a "foods tried" counter and a "this week" completion %. At the top, any auto-flagged allergens appear as a soft red banner.

📅

Plan

The full week-by-week plan as it was given to Mama. Each day is expandable — open any day to see quantity fed, mood, any reactions noted, photos of the actual meal, and parent notes. Past weeks are preserved, so you can audit any historical day.

🥬

Foods

The master list of vegetables, dals, and fruits. Items Daivik has tried get marked green. Items with reactions get the ⚠ flagged badge with a red border — so you instantly see what to avoid suggesting next week.

📊

Insights

The most useful tab for our consultations. Six charts:

  • Weekly completion — % of planned meals actually fed
  • Food variety — count of distinct vege/dal/fruit tried
  • Mood pattern — Loved / Okay / Fussy / Refused distribution
  • Growth over time — weight and length plotted against age
  • Recent reactions noted — chronological list of any reaction logged
  • Streak — consecutive days logged (mostly emotional, not clinical)
👶

Profile

Daivik's photo, age, full growth log (every weight/length entry, with notes), reminders, RDA references (6–12m), and the milestones checklist. Use this when you want to see the actual numbers behind the growth chart.

3 Editing the plan

The tools you have

✏️

Edit any meal

Open the Plan tab → tap any day card to expand → tap a meal name. Three buttons appear at the bottom of the meal: ✏️ Edit meal, ↪ Move, 🗑 Delete.

Edit lets you change the time of day, the food/dish name, and the recipe notes. Saves in place; logged details (Mama's quantity, photos, mood) are preserved.

↪

Move a meal to another day

From the same expanded meal, tap ↪ Move. A picker opens with a tab strip of all weeks and a grid of days. Tap any target day and the meal jumps there — along with everything Mama already logged for it (fed mark, qty, mood, reactions, photos, notes). The current day is greyed out.

🗑

Delete a meal

🗑 Delete removes the slot after a confirm. Any logged photos in Firebase Storage are cleaned up too. Other meals on the same day stay attached to Mama's logs (the indexes are renumbered automatically).

＋

Add a meal to a day

Scroll to the bottom of any expanded day card → + Add a meal to this day. Same modal as Edit: time, food, notes. New slot lands at the end of that day.

📥

Import the next week from Excel

Tap the round + button (bottom-right corner) → 📥 Import week (Excel) → pick your file. The app reads any sheet named "Week 8", "Week 9" etc. with columns Day · Time · Food · Notes and adds those weeks to the plan. If a week with that name exists, it's replaced (logs preserved).

✏️

Add a blank week manually

Same + button → ✏️ Add blank week. Useful if you don't have an Excel handy and want to scaffold the week directly in the app.

🥬

Add or remove foods from the master list

Open the Foods tab. At the top of each category (vege / dal / fruit) you'll see a small "+ Add" row — pick a section, type the new item, tap Add. Custom items render with a dashed border and a small ✕ to remove them later. Long-press any item to flag it (avoids in suggestions and shows ⚠ everywhere).

🛟 Safety net Every edit is just a Firestore write — it appears on Mama and Papa's phones within a second. There's no separate "save" step. If you change something by mistake, you can use the same Edit/Move/Delete tools to undo it. Mama can also re-import the previous week's Excel to overwrite a mangled week if needed.

4 What to look for

The data points that matter

⚠️

Auto-flagged foods

If a reaction is logged twice on the same food, the app auto-flags it. Look at the Today banner or the Foods tab to see the current list.

😣

Refused / Fussy streaks

Insights → Mood pattern. A spike in Refused/Fussy in one week usually means a texture or quantity issue.

📏

Growth trajectory

Insights → Growth chart. Compare against the trajectory; if it dips below his trend line for >2 weeks, worth flagging.

🥬

Diet diversity

Foods → tried items. Aim is 5+ of 8 food groups daily by week 7. The tab makes this visible.

📸

Photos of meals

Inside any expanded meal — photos show the actual texture and portion. Useful when "mashed" or "thick paste" is ambiguous.

📝

Parent notes

Often the richest signal — "grabbed the spoon", "gagged on the seeds", "refused after 2 tsp". Worth scanning the past week before each call.

🧠 A useful rhythm Before our weekly call, glance at: Today banner → Insights → Recent reactions → Growth chart → past 7 days in Plan. Total: ~3 minutes. Everything else is detail you can drill into if something looks off.

5 Common questions

If you wonder

What can I edit, and what can't I?

You can edit anything to do with the plan: meals (add / edit / move / delete), entire weeks (import or build manually), and the master food list. You cannot mark a meal as "fed", add quantities, mood, photos, or text notes — those are Mama and Papa's daily log. You also cannot edit Daivik's growth measurements or profile photo. The app hides those buttons so the daily-log surface stays clean for them.

Where exactly are the edit buttons?

Plan tab → tap a day card to open it → tap a meal name to expand it. Three buttons at the bottom of the meal: Edit / Move / Delete. To add a meal: scroll to the bottom of the expanded day → + Add a meal to this day. To add a whole week: round + button at the bottom-right of any screen → Import week (Excel) or Add blank week.

If I edit something, when does Mama see it?

Within ~1 second. The app uses Firestore realtime sync — every save you make pushes to all signed-in devices immediately. No refresh, no "save and send" step.

Can I undo an edit?

There's no global undo, but every action is reversible with the same tools: edit a meal back, move it back to the original day (logs travel with it), or re-import the previous Excel to overwrite a week. Tell Mama if you want a sanity check.

How real-time is the sync?

About 1 second. When Mama marks a meal fed, it updates on your screen within a moment, and vice versa.

What if I see a worrying reaction?

The app auto-flags repeated reactions, but obviously call/message Mama directly if you see something serious in the Recent reactions list. The app is a record, not an alert system.

Can I see what Daivik ate 4 weeks ago?

Yes — the Plan tab keeps every past week. Use the week selector (top of the Plan tab) to jump back.

Do my edits or views show up to anyone as activity?

The app stamps each cloud write with the editing role (mom/dad/dietician) so Mayank can audit if needed, but there's no notification or "dietician edited X" feed. You can browse and edit quietly.

What if I forget the PIN?

Ask Mayank — he can reset it for you in a minute.
