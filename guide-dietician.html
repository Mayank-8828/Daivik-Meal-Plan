1 Getting in

Signing in & staying signed in

📱

Where to find it

Open mealplan.mayankkariya.com in your browser. On the welcome screen, tap the 🩺 Dietician card and enter the 4-digit PIN we shared with you.

You stay signed in until you sign out — works on phone, tablet, laptop. Add it to your home screen for a one-tap launch (browser menu → "Add to Home Screen").

💡 Privacy note Your sign-in is yours alone — Mama and Papa each have their own account, as do other family members. Anything you "view" is not visible to them as activity. The PIN is shareable only if you decide to share it.

2 What you can see

The five tabs, at a glance

🏠

Today

What Daivik is supposed to eat today, with a tick next to each meal that's been fed. Has a "foods tried" counter and a "this week" completion %. At the top, any auto-flagged allergens appear as a soft red banner.

📅

Plan

The full week-by-week plan as it was given to Mama. Each day is expandable — open any day to see quantity fed, mood, any reactions noted, photos of the actual meal, and parent notes. Past weeks are preserved, so you can audit any historical day.

🥬

Foods

The master list of vegetables, dals, and fruits. Items Daivik has tried get marked green. Items with reactions get the ⚠ flagged badge with a red border — so you instantly see what to avoid suggesting next week.

📊

Insights

The most useful tab for our consultations. Six charts:

  • Weekly completion — % of planned meals actually fed
  • Food variety — count of distinct vege/dal/fruit tried
  • Mood pattern — Loved / Okay / Fussy / Refused distribution
  • Growth over time — weight and length plotted against age
  • Recent reactions noted — chronological list of any reaction logged
  • Streak — consecutive days logged (mostly emotional, not clinical)
👶

Profile

Daivik's photo, age, full growth log (every weight/length entry, with notes), reminders, RDA references (6–12m), and the milestones checklist. Use this when you want to see the actual numbers behind the growth chart.

3 What to look for

The data points that matter

⚠️

Auto-flagged foods

If a reaction is logged twice on the same food, the app auto-flags it. Look at the Today banner or the Foods tab to see the current list.

😣

Refused / Fussy streaks

Insights → Mood pattern. A spike in Refused/Fussy in one week usually means a texture or quantity issue.

📏

Growth trajectory

Insights → Growth chart. Compare against the trajectory; if it dips below his trend line for >2 weeks, worth flagging.

🥬

Diet diversity

Foods → tried items. Aim is 5+ of 8 food groups daily by week 7. The tab makes this visible.

📸

Photos of meals

Inside any expanded meal — photos show the actual texture and portion. Useful when "mashed" or "thick paste" is ambiguous.

📝

Parent notes

Often the richest signal — "grabbed the spoon", "gagged on the seeds", "refused after 2 tsp". Worth scanning the past week before each call.

🧠 A useful rhythm Before our weekly call, glance at: Today banner → Insights → Recent reactions → Growth chart → past 7 days in Plan. Total: ~3 minutes. Everything else is detail you can drill into if something looks off.

4 Sending the next week

How new plans land

📤

The way it works today

You send Mama the next week's plan as an Excel sheet on WhatsApp, just like before. She imports it with one tap from the app and the new week shows up here for you to see too.

Format that works best: one sheet per week named "Week 8", "Week 9", etc. Columns: Day, Time, Food, Notes. The app handles parsing automatically.

📥

If you want a weekly digest

Mama has a 📤 Share button that builds a clean text summary of the last 7 days — meals, moods, reactions, growth — and sends it via WhatsApp or email. Ask her to use it before each consult if you'd like everything in one message.

5 Common questions

If you wonder

Can I edit anything?

No — the dietician account is intentionally view-only. If you want a change made (a flag corrected, a wrong log fixed), just tell Mama or Papa and they'll do it.

How real-time is the sync?

About 1 second. When Mama marks a meal fed, it updates on your screen within a moment. No refresh needed.

What if I see a worrying reaction?

The app auto-flags repeated reactions, but obviously call/message Mama directly if you see something serious in the Recent reactions list. The app is a record, not an alert system.

Can I see what Daivik ate 4 weeks ago?

Yes — the Plan tab keeps every past week. Use the week selector (top of the Plan tab) to jump back.

Do my views show up to anyone?

No. There's no activity tracking — you can browse freely.

What if I forget the PIN?

Ask Mayank — he can reset it for you in a minute.
